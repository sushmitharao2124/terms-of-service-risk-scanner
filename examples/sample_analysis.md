## 📝 Terms of Service Analysis

### 📖 Plain English Summary
* **You lose control of your data**: Any content you upload can be used, modified, and shared by the company forever without paying you.
* **Your privacy is compromised**: The company can share your identifiable information, location, and browsing habits with marketers.
* **You can't sue as a group**: You are not allowed to join a class-action lawsuit and must resolve disputes through private arbitration.
* **Auto-agreement**: Using the app means you agree to all terms.

### 🚩 Red Flags Detected
* **[🔴 HIGH] Perpetual Data License**: Clause 2 claims an irrevocable, royalty-free right to all user-uploaded content.
* **[🔴 HIGH] Third-Party Data Sharing**: Clause 3 allows sharing location and identifiable info for marketing without additional consent.
* **[🟡 MODERATE] Forced Arbitration**: Clause 4 forces disputes into a private court and waives class action rights.

**Verdict**: ⚠️ Proceed with caution. You are giving up significant privacy and intellectual property rights.
